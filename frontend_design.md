# Frontend Design System

## Core Aesthetic
*   **Theme:** Minimalist Black & White.
*   **Shapes:** Highly rounded corners (`rounded-2xl`, `rounded-3xl`) to contrast the rigid typography.
*   **Typography:** Old Typewriter Font (`Courier Prime` or similar monospace) for headings and UI accents, paired with a clean geometric sans (`Inter`) for dense data readability.
*   **Accents:** Subtle grays / stark black borders.

## Layout Structure
*   **Sidebar:** Left-aligned, cleanly separated from the main content.
*   **Content Area:** Off-white background to make the stark white cards pop.
*   **Cards:** Thick padding, rounded corners, subtle shadows or solid borders.
